Supported geometry formats

  • HEC-RAS geometry files (commonly .g01, .g02, …) that contain Junct Name= and Reach XY= blocks.
  • HEC-HMS basin/geometry text files (commonly .basin) that include Canvas X: and Canvas Y: attributes for elements.

If your file uses a different structure, the app will still display the raw contents and try best-effort parsing, but junction/pipe extraction may be limited.
